Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Geoffrey Serdar and Serdar+Partners at Compass ("we," "us") collect and use the information you provide on geoffreyserdar.com.

Information we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the information you choose to provide — your name, phone number, and the type of inquiry you select. We also note how you reached our site (for example, an online ad or a link we shared) so we can understand what's working.

How we use it

  • To contact you about your real estate inquiry by phone call and text message.
  • To respond to your questions and provide the services you've asked about.
  • To understand which of our marketing efforts bring people to our site.

We do not sell your personal information.

Text messaging & consent

By submitting our contact form, you agree that we may contact you by phone call and text message (including by automated means) at the number you provide, regarding your real estate inquiry. Consent is not a condition of any purchase. Message and data rates may apply, and message frequency varies. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out of text messages, or HELP for assistance.

No mobile information or text-messaging opt-in data will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text-messaging consent is not shared with any third parties.

How we protect it

Your information stays with Geoffrey and is used only to follow up on your inquiry. We retain it only as long as needed for that purpose or as required by law.

Your choices

You may opt out of text messages anytime by replying STOP. To ask what information we have, to correct it, or to have it deleted, contact us using the details below.
